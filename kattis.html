Kattis Problems

Kattis is a webite that hosts practice and competition Problems for competetive programming. Competetive Programming is a team game where each team has limited time to efficiently solve well defined math and coding problems in many languages. Compiled here are the solutions to the Kattis problems I have solved while being a member on the University of Louisville ICPC team.
